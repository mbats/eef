Obeo

Obeo is a high-tech company that provides open software solutions to create and transform complex systems.

  • Industrial systems (avionics, space, defense, energy, transportation, etc.)
  • IT applications
  • Digital transformation of large companies.

As a major Open Source player, Obeo is a strategic member of the Eclipse foundation (alongside IBM, Oracle, SAP, Google) and a member of The Open Group.
